Try patterns like \bt\w*\b to find words starting with 't' 
[]

Find words starting with 't'

\bt\w*\b

Example text: "The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"

Find email addresses

\b[A-Za-z0-9._%+-]+@[A-Za-z0-9.-]+\.[A-Za-z]{2,}\b

Example text: "Contact me at john.doe@example.com or support@company.org"

Find dates (YYYY-MM-DD)

\b\d{4}-\d{2}-\d{2}\b

Example text: "The event is on 2024-04-23 and ends on 2024-04-25"

Find all numbers

\b\d+\b

Example text: "There are 42 apples and 7 oranges in 3 baskets"
